Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 01 Jan 2025

Welcome to HyloShip. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our shipping and logistics platform.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal details such as name, email, phone number
  • Business and pickup address details
  • Shipment, order, and tracking information
  • Technical data such as IP address and browser details

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide shipping and fulfillment services
  • To process orders, payments, and COD remittances
  • To improve platform performance and customer support
  • To comply with legal and regulatory requirements

3. Information Sharing

We may share your data with courier partners, payment gateways, and government authorities when required. We do not sell your data.

4. Data Security

HyloShip follows industry-standard security practices, but no system can guarantee complete security.

5. Cookies

Cookies help us improve your experience. You may disable cookies in your browser settings.

6. Contact Us

For privacy-related concerns, email us at support@hyloship.com
