    Frequently Asked Questions

    You can find your AWB (Air Waybill) number or order ID in the order confirmation email or SMS sent by the merchant. Once you have it, you can track your shipment directly on the HyloShip tracking page.

    If your tracking number isn’t showing updates, it may be because the shipment is yet to be picked up by the courier or the tracking system hasn’t updated yet. Please wait for a few hours and try again. If the issue persists, contact HyloShip support with your AWB number.

    Delays can occur due to weather, logistics, or courier issues. You can track your shipment in real-time using your AWB number. For further assistance, contact HyloShip support and provide your tracking details.

    If your shipment shows as delivered but you haven’t received it, immediately contact the merchant and HyloShip support. Provide your AWB number so that we can investigate with the courier partner.

    Packages can be delayed due to high shipment volumes, courier disruptions, or address-related issues. You can track your order live via HyloShip tracking. For detailed updates, contact HyloShip support.

    HyloShip handles shipping and delivery. To return an order, you need to get in touch with the merchant from whom you purchased the product. They will provide return instructions, and HyloShip can assist with the pickup if scheduled.

    If your shipment has been returned or cancelled, please contact the merchant for further details on refund or rescheduling delivery. You may also reach out to HyloShip support for assistance in tracking the return.
